Welcome to Botanify Tech!

Our Plant Scanner application leverages advanced AI to identify various plants and provide comprehensive care information. Whether you're a seasoned gardener or a plant enthusiast, our app will help you take better care of your plants with ease.

Key Features

  • Plant Identification: Simply scan a plant using your smartphone camera, and our AI will instantly recognize the species
  • Care Instructions: Receive detailed information on how to care for the identified plant, including watering, sunlight, soil type, and common issues
  • Maintenance Tips: Get personalized tips and reminders to ensure your plants stay healthy and vibrant.
  • Database Access: Access a vast database of plants, including flowers, shrubs, trees, and indoor plants.
